# 2. What is Air Quality Index (AQI)?

### Explanation

AQI is a number that tells us how clean or polluted the air is.

It helps people understand whether the air is safe to breathe.

Higher AQI means worse air quality.

Example:

| AQI Range | Category     |
| --------- | ------------ |
| 0–50      | Good         |
| 51–100    | Satisfactory |
| 101–200   | Moderate     |
| 201–300   | Poor         |
| 301–400   | Very Poor    |
| 401–500   | Severe       |

### Explain in review

> AQI converts complex pollution measurements into one easy number so everyone can understand the quality of air.
